Muhamad Rizky Nurdiyanto

Muhamad Rizky Nurdiyanto is a professional Sound Utility actively working in the Indonesian film industry since 2023. With 16 credits across films and web series, he has contributed across various genres — from action and horror to drama and comedy.

His experience spans roles as sound utility, wireless sound operator, boom operator, and assistant sound recordist — demonstrating broad capability across the entire sound department.

Among his notable works is The Shadow Strays (Netflix, 2024), which reached the Netflix Top 10 in 85 countries, and Agak Laen: Menyala Pantiku! (2025) with an IMDb rating of 7.8.
